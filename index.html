About PharmChain

Fighting Counterfeit Drugs with Blockchain

PharmChain is a distributed ledger platform designed to eliminate fake drugs from the pharmaceutical supply chain through cryptographic verification, real-time tracking, and transparent record-keeping.

According to the WHO, ~10% of medical products in developing markets are falsified. In Nigeria, NAFDAC estimates up to 25% of circulating drugs may not be genuine. Our blockchain makes fraud mathematically impossible.

Every registered drug gets a unique SHA-256 cryptographic hash. Any tampering ‚Äî even a single character ‚Äî produces a completely different hash, instantly detectable across all network nodes.

üéØ Our Mission

To eliminate counterfeit drugs from Nigeria's supply chain through immutable blockchain technology, protecting lives and restoring trust.

üî≠ Our Vision

To become Africa's leading pharmaceutical blockchain network, setting the gold standard for drug authentication and supply chain transparency.

SHA-256 cryptographic hashing, distributed ledger consensus, and real-time GPS tracking ensure every drug's chain of custody is permanent and tamper-proof.
